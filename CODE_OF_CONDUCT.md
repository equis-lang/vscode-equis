# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a professional and welcoming environment for all contributors. Our goal is to foster a community focused on technical excellence and the advancement of high-integrity financial systems.

## Standards
- Use professional and inclusive language in all communications.
- Be respectful of differing technical opinions and approaches.
- Focus on constructive feedback during code reviews.
- Gracefully accept technical criticism.

## Responsibility
Maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including the issue tracker, pull requests, and any official community forums or chat rooms.
